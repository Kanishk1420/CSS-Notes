    • Topic 3.1- Margin Collapse

  • Margin Collapse:- When two margins from different elements overlap,the equivalent margin is the greater of the two. this is called Margin Collapse.

  • Ex:- Suppose box1 margin contain 30px margin and box2 margin contain 30 px so box1 and box2 both will share above 30-30px margin.

  • If box1 margin has 30px and box2 contain 20px then box2 will share the greater margin {30px} or box1 margin and box2 margin will Collapse.
